SET_FLIGHTPLAN_SWITCH

This command configures a 2- or 3-position switch on your RC-remote to be used to switch between different BLOCKs in your flight plan. When the RC-switch changes position, the configured line in the flightplan becomes the active one. There are 3 positions to be configured. When you only have a 2-position switch, you can ignore the middle configuration option.

  • Channel: The PWM or PPM input channel number that is the RC-switch.

  • Line < 1.4ms (left): Jump to the line when the switch goes to the "low" position.

  • Line < 1.6ms (center): Jump to the line when the switch goes to the "middle" position. Change to the standby line if you have a 2-position switch. (don't forget to test)

  • Line > 1.6ms (right): Jump to the line when the switch goes to the "high" position.
